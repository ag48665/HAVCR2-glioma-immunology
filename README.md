\# HAVCR2-glioma-immunology



Integrated TCGA and CGGA analysis of HAVCR2, immune infiltration, macrophage enrichment, and prognosis in glioma.



\## Main findings



\- High immune infiltration is associated with poor survival in TCGA gliomas.

\- HAVCR2 is strongly associated with immune infiltration.

\- High HAVCR2 expression predicts worse survival in the CGGA validation cohort.

\- HAVCR2 expression is elevated in WHO grade IV and IDH-wildtype gliomas.

\- HAVCR2 strongly correlates with macrophage marker scores in both TCGA and CGGA.
